About

My name is Josh. I came up with this concept, designed it, coded the front end and the smart contract, and handled all of the company formation. If something is broken, it's my fault. If it works well, let me know!

My hope is to make enough money to build a team around me and build this project into a decentralized social network.

Why a social network? Immediately after graduating high school I moved to NYC and founded a social network with some friends. I was responsible for building the "retention" mechanisms (ie. dopamine hacking). I read papers such as Facebook's Social Contagion paper. Then I watched Cambridge Analytica use the same tools to swing an election.

It became clear to me that centralized control of information was very dangerous. This was before films, like the Social Dilemma, and books, like Surveillance Capitalism, brought this problem into public awareness so I felt very alone in my fear of the problem.

Further, I believe that most people underestimate the danger of this problem. As phones become read/write BCIs and social networks become swarms of autonomous robots, the danger of centralization will heighten. I believe that crypto will provide a way to address this problem. My hope is to build decentralized information systems that help to keep you, not Musk or Zuck or Biden, in control of the brakes on your Tesla.

1) I'm starting with a viral product, based off The Million Dollar Homepage, 2) expanding this into a social network DAO, and 3) hopefully expanding that beyond inter-human communication to inter-robotic communication.
